The Story Behind

Almost twenty years ago, 2007, the physics students Niclas Moldenhauer, Moritz Feyerabend, Enno Harms and Hinnrich Thehinnie founded the "Olfrat" in a constitutional assembly in the Web 2.0 tower in Braunschweig. Inspired by the unwavering happiness and optimism of one of their co-students, Olf, especially in situations of intense stress or great danger, the main objective of the newly founded organization was to spread the philosophy of life that Olf demonstrated without knowing. In essence, this philosophy fosters conscious gratitude about the good aspects of life and a stoic view on the challenges and hurdles to be taken by everyone. This is mostly achieved by a mindset of steady learning and training of both body and soul, with the ultimate goal to become a more conscious and capable human being.

Today's Business

After the initial period of a community that gathered for fun and games, a business was formed that professionalizes the effort on improving the lives of normal citizens by researching intelligent digital solutions for day-to-day problems. The core values of our team still orient today on the philosophy shaped by Olf 20 years ago: fun, growth, and happiness.
